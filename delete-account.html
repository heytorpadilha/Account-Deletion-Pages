This page explains how users can request deletion of their JGlic account and personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, send an email to:

Email: heytor.dev@gmail.com

Please include the email address used to log in to the JGlic app so we can locate and remove your data.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your JGlic account
  • Email associated with the account
  • All glucose records
  • All alarms and reminder settings
  • Profile information

What May Be Temporarily Retained

  • Security logs (kept for up to 90 days)
  • Error logs for stability improvements

Deletion Timeline

Your account and data will be fully deleted within 30 days after receiving your email request.

This page applies to the app JGlic, published by developer Heytor Thompson Padilha Bastos.
